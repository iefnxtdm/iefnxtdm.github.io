About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL), working on advanced building thermal technologies. I develop thermal switches for dynamic insulation, characterize novel cement-based insulation claddings, and apply analytical and numerical models to validate experiments. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. in Civil Engineering at WPI.

Interests: dynamic envelopes, thermal switches, SS-PCM, radiative cooling, EnergyPlus co-simulation, and PINN/ML for inverse heat transfer.
